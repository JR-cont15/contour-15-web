About Us

Contour 15 is the dedicated sales agency representing Black Crows skis. We work closely with specialty ski retailers to bring the full Black Crows line to shops that share a passion for craftsmanship, performance, and the mountain experience.

Our team understands the product inside and out, and we're here to support retailers with everything from seasonal line presentations to in-store support and sell-through strategies.

What We Do

Trade Shows

As active members of 360 Collective and EWSRA, we attend regional winter shows and provide seasonal previews of the full Black Crows collection, helping retailers build the right assortment for their market.

Retail Support

On snow demos, Staff training, product knowledge clinics, and merchandising guidance to drive sell-through on the shop floor.

Account Management

Dedicated partnership with each retailer, from order planning through delivery and beyond.
